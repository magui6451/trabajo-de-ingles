Should / Shouldn't

Should:

  • Recycle plastic, glass, and paper.
  • Plant trees to protect nature.
  • Save water and electricity.
  • Participate in community clean-ups.

Shouldn't:

  • Use single-use plastic products.
  • Litter in natural environments.
  • Waste food or resources.
  • Ignore pollution warnings.

Must / Mustn't

Must:

  • Protect endangered species.
  • Support clean energy solutions.
  • Participate in community clean-ups.
  • Educate others about environmental issues.

Mustn't:

  • Cut down forests illegally.
  • Ignore pollution warnings.
  • Destroy wildlife habitats.
  • Overconsume natural resources.
